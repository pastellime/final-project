#001 返魂香 HANGONKO

Hangonko is a kind of incense medicine with the effect of reviving the dead, based on the strigot tree, which can continue to emit a very strong aroma, it is said that it can float hundreds of miles, and those who are less than three days dead can smell this incense and come to life, and its smoke can recall the spirits of the dead, so that the living can reunite with the dead.
