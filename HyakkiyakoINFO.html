HYAKKIYAKO INFORMATION

#001 返魂香 (返魂香) Hongonko

Hongonko is a kind of incense medicine with the effect of reviving the dead, based on the strigot tree, which can continue to emit a very strong aroma, it is said that it can float hundreds of miles, and those who are less than three days dead can smell this incense and come to life, and its smoke can recall the spirits of the dead, so that the living can reunite with the dead

#002 青行燈 (青行灯) Aoandon

Before the "百物语" game, the participants gathered in the same dark room. After each of them had finished a strange story to tell in turn, they had to leave their seats in the dark and go into the next room where the walking lamp was burning and blow out a wick. After the ninety-ninth strange talk, the last wick was left to continue burning. If it comes to the hundredth strange talk, Aoandon will appear.

#003 煙々羅 (烟烟罗) Enenra

Enenra is a monster with scary faces in the smoke. "In the quiet home mosquito-repellent incense around the smoke, make a strange shape. Just as thin clothes are blown by the wind, so they are called Enenra."
